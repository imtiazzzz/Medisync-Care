INTEGRATED ADMIN PANEL

Many healthcare institutions today struggle to meet the necessary standards for patient satisfaction and effective complaint resolution, often due to the lack of an integrated hospital management platform. Recognizing this challenge, we have proactively addressed it.

Introducing our Integrated Admin Panel, a solution designed to oversee and manage patient and doctor-related matters efficiently. This platform connects authorities, patients, and staff directly, ensuring transparency, accountability, and a fair approach to healthcare management.

FACILITIES FOR THE ELDERLY ONES

Recognizing the unique needs of our elderly patients, we have implemented several features to ensure their comfort and convenience.

These include priority appointment scheduling, easy access to their medical history and detailed reports with a single click, and personalized supervision through video consultations (for those unable to visit in person). These initiatives set a new standard for providing exceptional care to our elderly patients.

VIDEO CONFERENCING FACILITIES

During the challenging times of COVID-19, it has become increasingly difficult for patients, especially the elderly, to attend regular check-ups in person.

To address this, we have introduced an innovative solution—Over the Call Supervision (OCS). This feature allows patients to consult with doctors via video conferencing, ensuring that their healthcare needs are met without the need to leave their homes.

MEASURES FOR COVID-19

We recognize that COVID-19 patients are among the most vulnerable and require specialized care, as well as isolation from other patients for effective treatment.

Committed to transparency, we provide our patients with access to real-time COVID statistics. Additionally, we have established a dedicated COVID ward with up-to-date records of available beds, vaccination status, and patient history, all meticulously tracked to ensure the best possible care.
